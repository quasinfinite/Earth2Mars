About Earth2Mars

The Earth2Mars supports the notion that expanding the human experience to Mars is essential to furthering ourselves as a species. Earth2Mars hopes to bring this idea to the forefront of society through style.

Materials are also very important to us at Earth2Mars. A percentage of every purchase goes to support organic farms around the nation. A number of the farms we help support happen to grow cotton. We use this relationship with farmers to provide specially grown, organic cotton with which we use to make these shirts.
